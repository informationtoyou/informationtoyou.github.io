Pricing

We teach Math & Geography for €5/hour, currently we have an offer, for the first 3 classes, you can pay €2.50/hour. If you have 1 class/week, you can purchase a monthly plan, with 4 classes for €20 and if you want to have 2 classes a week for a month, the plan would be €40. You can pay-as-you-go or pay-in-advance, your preference.

Why choose us?

My lessons offer flexibility and every 2 classes, I can talk to the parent(s)/guardian(s) about the progress and performance of their child. I will give assignments/homework that isn't boring but rather interesting, helpful and increases your child's knowledge. Since many kids have pressure at school already, under the parent's request I can lower the homework, or not give it. I have a tailor-made curriculum to help your child learn, not just quickly, but efficiently. This enables your child to have a conceptual understanding. So, no, my classes are not jokes, they are there to help you. I will give visual examples using an online whiteboard and I will use Worksheets to quiz the student during class.

How do the classes work?

I have a curriculum for Grades 1-3 in Math & Geography, not just that, if your child is struggling with math in school I can help them with their concepts and/or homework. All the classes are taken on Zoom/Google Meet, whatever you prefer (personally I prefer if the student uses Google Meet as there is no time limit of 40 mins, but If you would like to use Zoom, feel free to). You can send an e-mail to learnwithatharva@gmail.com and we will try to respond as fast as possible and we will email you with following steps, such as payment information, when can you book the classes & more.
